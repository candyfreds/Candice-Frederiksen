Speak Up

About

Greenway Arts Alliance in association with Da Poetry Lounge presents the 3rd Annual LA Get Down Festival, celebrating spoken word and hip-hop. The festival features local poets, artists, and tons of events/workshops.

Audience

The primary audience for Speak Up ranges from youths to adults interested in hearing, learning, or performing spoken word and hip-hop. The festival encourages all individuals regardless of age, race, or sexuality to participate, learn, and be heard!
