Drumming Pioneer

Booklet Design

About

The PDX Food Truck Booklet is a collection of local food trucks around the city of Portland, Oregon. Each food truck has it's own food type as well as unique skills and recipes that help make up the best food in town.

Audience

The primary audience for this booklet is foodie locals or tourists interesting in learning more about what local food trucks have to offer. It is a great way to learn more about the city along with details about each food truck and the best food option they have available.

Branding

This was a playful experiment with page layout along with data organization. I wanted to use color in a way that excited the users tastebuds and craving for food while reading. Each food truck had a unique food option in which I illustrated in order to give a more visual representation rather than focusing on words.
